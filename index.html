Extracurriculars

BadgerBOTS Robotics

Head of Programming & Scouting • 2013-2018

The FIRST Robotics Competition os an opportunity for students to work together to solve a common problem. We design (CAD), build and program our robot which has advanced to the global competition the past two years. My sophomore year, I developed a web app that allowed our team to gather competitive data with our phones instead of paper. It used php and databases to store the data. The system also compiles and generalizes the data so that we can quickly and effectively determine team strategy. This year, I am leading the overall programming effort (6 teammates) for the team. Last summer, I led an effort to build a 3d printer.

Rocket Club

Lead Payload Monitoring System Engineer • 2017-Present

The rocket club is part of the Rockets for Schools program which builds a 7-foot rocket with a payload inside. Our team will participate in a launch with other teams and industry professionals. My role is to lead a subgroup focused on developing a comprehensive monitoring system. This will include sensors that collect temperature, rotation, pressure etc. and will log the data to an onboard computer. We are using C++ to program the onboard computer. This data will later be used to assess the effectiveness of the wind generators we have added to the rocket.
